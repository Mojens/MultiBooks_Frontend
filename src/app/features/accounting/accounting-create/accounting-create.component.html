Register Purchase

Registering a cash purchase requires you to fill in details about the transaction completed with immediate payment. This includes the date of the document, the entity or person from whom you bought the items (Bought From), and the financial details of the transaction. These financial details comprise the subtotal with VAT, the subtotal without VAT, and the total amount of the purchase. This process is essential for keeping accurate records of all cash-based transactions.

When registering a credit purchase, you'll need to provide details about the transaction made on credit terms. This includes the date of the document, the due date for payment, the currency used (Valuta), and the supplier. You also need to fill in the entity or person from whom the items were bought (Bought From), along with the financial details such as the subtotal with VAT, the subtotal without VAT, and the total amount of the purchase. Recording credit purchases accurately is crucial for managing payables and understanding your financial obligations.
